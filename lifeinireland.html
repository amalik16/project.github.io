How is Life in Ireland?

Ireland is a small country in Europe, which speaks English, as common language and Irish as a national language and is famous for the hospitality and its vast culture, including some festival, the most famous being the St. Patrick's Day. It's a good place for Education purposes and many people come to the country to learn English, visiting the beautiful nature and drink the great dark Guinness beer.

Dublin Weather

Dublin is mostly cold, the summer is short, but it does not scary people away from going out and it hardly snows in the country. The pubs are always warm! Don't forget to go out with your jacket :)

Jobs in Dublin

Students can work 20 hours per week during the Studies and 40 hours per week in the holidays. It is very easy for a student to get some job and taking this opportunity for some pocket money and also improving English.

Studying in Dublin

In Griffith College, there are courses for every students, from Business to Computing, Journalism, Arts... the list is very long. Also, English courses for those who are not yet very comfortable about the language.

Places in Ireland

The nature in Ireland is beautiful. There are many cliffs to visit and even the Parks inside Dublin. They offer a wide variety of trees and birds. Also, the history of the country is wonderful, with old castles and pubs to visit.
